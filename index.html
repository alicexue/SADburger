SADBurger




Welcome to SADBurger!

Created by Sally Bao, Alice Xue, and Derrick Lui

Instructions:

Press the left and right arrow keys to move your bun.
Catch the patties, but avoid the burnt ones.
Be careful as the stack can tilt if uneven!

Points:

Meat = 5 points
Cheese = 4 points
Tomato = 3 points
Lettuce = 2 points
Burnt Meat = -3 points
